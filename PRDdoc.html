1. Introduction

1.1 Purpose

The purpose of this document is to outline the requirements for the NexGen Video Surveillance Management Studio. This document provides detailed information about the product's objectives, features, and specifications to ensure alignment with business goals and user needs.

1.2 Scope

This document covers the development of an advanced video surveillance management system with real-time monitoring, AI-based analytics, multi-camera support, and robust data storage solutions. It includes both the front-end and backend components, AI models, and integration capabilities.

2. Project Overview

2.1 Vision

To revolutionize video surveillance by providing an intelligent, integrated, and intuitive platform that enhances security, streamlines management, and empowers users with advanced analytics and automation.

2.2 Goals

  • Enhance security and safety
  • Streamline video surveillance management
  • Provide advanced analytics and actionable insights
  • Enable seamless integration with existing systems
  • Ensure a user-friendly interface and experience

3. Business Case

3.1 Business Opportunity/Need

Organizations need efficient, scalable, and intelligent video surveillance solutions to enhance security and operational efficiency. This project aims to meet these demands by providing a comprehensive solution.

3.2 Problem Statement

Current video surveillance systems often lack the capability to handle large-scale environments and provide real-time AI-based analysis. The NexGen Video Surveillance Management Studio aims to overcome these challenges by offering a robust and scalable solution.

5. Requirements

5.1 Functional Requirements

  • Real-time video analytics
  • AI-driven incident detection and alerts
  • Seamless integration with third-party systems
  • Scalable cloud-based storage
  • Intuitive user interface with customizable dashboards
  • User authentication and role-based access control

5.2 Non-Functional Requirements

  • Performance: Must handle high concurrency and provide quick response times.
  • Security: Must ensure data privacy and comply with regulations like GDPR.
  • Scalability: Support for large-scale environments with multiple cameras.
  • Reliability: High availability and minimal downtime.
  • Usability: Easy to use with a minimal learning curve.

7. User Interface and Experience

7.1 Design Principles

  • Consistency: Maintain a uniform look and feel across all screens.
  • Simplicity: Avoid unnecessary complexity.
  • Feedback: Provide clear and immediate feedback for user actions.
  • Accessibility: Ensure the interface is accessible to all users.

7.2 UI Mockups and Prototypes

  • High-fidelity mockups of key screens
  • Interactive prototypes for user testing

8. Technical Requirements

8.1 Technology Stack

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React/Vue.js
  • Backend: Node.js/Java, RESTful APIs
  • Database: MongoDB/PostgreSQL
  • AI/ML: Python, TensorFlow/PyTorch
  • Deployment: Docker, Kubernetes, AWS/GCP

8.2 Performance Requirements

  • System must support at least 1000 concurrent users.
  • Response time for critical operations must be under 2 seconds.

8.3 Security Requirements

  • Data encryption in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Compliance with GDPR and other relevant regulations

9. Assumptions

  • Users have access to devices with internet connectivity.
  • The organization has the necessary infrastructure to support the surveillance management studio.
  • Stakeholders will provide timely feedback during development.
  • Third-party services used for translation and other functionalities will remain operational.
