Agile Transformation

Retail SaaS Platform

Overview: Led the agile transformation of a retail SaaS platform, focusing on team empowerment, agile practices, and continuous delivery.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Implemented SAFe practices to align teams with agile principles.
  • Facilitated PI planning sessions and sprint retrospectives.
  • Empowered teams by fostering a culture of collaboration and continuous improvement.

Achievements:

  • Successfully transformed the development process, reducing time-to-market by 40%.
  • Increased team productivity and morale through effective agile practices.
  • Enhanced product quality and customer satisfaction by implementing continuous delivery.

Agile Transformation Plan:

  • PI Planning: Implement PI planning to align teams on goals and objectives.

  • Foster a collaborative environment where team members feel valued and heard.
  • Encourage cross-functional partnerships to leverage diverse expertise.
  • Implement feedback loops to ensure continuous learning and adaptation.

Results:

  • Improved customer satisfaction scores by 20%.
  • Enhanced team collaboration and communication, leading to more innovative solutions.
